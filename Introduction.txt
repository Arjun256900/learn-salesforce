Introduction: 

- Founded in 1999, California, by Marc Benioff
- Cloud based CRM platform
- First off, spanned CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and SaaS.
- Later expanded to PaaS as well. Now it spans a wide range of domains including CRM, Analytics, integration, and collaboration.
- Multi-tenant architecture; kind of like same workspace, but every user is isolated and secured.
- Meta-data driven

Key components:

There are a few clouds used for specific use-cases. 

1. Sales cloud (Manage standard salesforce objects)
2. Service cloud
3. Commerce cloud (B2B and B2C)
4. Marketing cloud
5. Analytics cloud
